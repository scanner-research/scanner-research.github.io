Publications¶

Scanner is an active research project, part of a collaboration between Carnegie Mellon and Stanford University. Please contact Alex Poms and Will Crichton with questions.

Paper citation¶

Scanner was published at SIGGRAPH 2018 as “Scanner: Efficient Video Analysis at Scale by Poms, Crichton, Hanrahan, and Fatahalian. If you use Scanner in your research, we’d appreciate it if you cite the paper.
